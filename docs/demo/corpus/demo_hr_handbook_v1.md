# People Ops Handbook v1

## Leave and time off

Demo Company grants India-based employees 16 weeks of paid parental leave for
primary caregivers and 8 weeks for secondary caregivers. Employees outside
India receive 12 weeks for primary caregivers and 6 weeks for secondary
caregivers unless a local law requires more. PTO carryover is capped at 5 days
and carried days expire on March 31 of the following calendar year.

## Remote work

Most teams use Tuesday and Thursday as anchor days for live collaboration.
Employees may work remotely on Monday, Wednesday, and Friday unless their
manager marks a role as location-critical. A manager can approve temporary
remote work for up to 30 calendar days.

## Stipends and travel

Every employee has a 350 USD annual ergonomic stipend approved by People Ops.
Travel expenses over 250 USD require both manager approval and Finance review.
Reimbursements must be submitted within 30 days of the expense date.

## Performance process

Formal performance reviews happen in June and December. Managers should record
promotion feedback in the review tool and keep calibration notes inside the
restricted People Ops drive.
